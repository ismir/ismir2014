Best Oral/Poster Presenter Award

At ISMIR 2014, the Best Presenter Award will be given to two presenters (oral and poster). The awardees will be selected by popular vote. You will find your ballot in the end of the conference booklet. After making your selection, please remember to drop off your ballot in a box set up at the registration desk. Please cast your vote before the banquet (Thursday night). Each awardee will be given a certificate and an ASUS PadFone S (smartphone).

  • Best oral presentation: "Analyzing Song Structure with Spectral Clustering," Brian McFee and Dan Ellis.
  • Best poster presentation: "MIR_EVAL: A Transparent Implementation of Common MIR Metrics," Colin Raffel, Brian McFee, Eric J. Humphrey, Justin Salamon, Oriol Nieto, Dawen Liang, Daniel P. W. Ellis.
