Registration
Registration Info
Participants should register via the online registration system: http://eform.iis.sinica.edu.tw/url/ismir2014 .

Important notice: For each paper/music piece, at least one author must be registered for the conference by 25th July 2014. If none of the authors is registered by this date, the paper/music piece will be removed from the conference proceedings and programm.

Invitation Letter
If you need an invitation letter, after completing your registration and payment, send a request by e-mail to
ismir2014-registration@ismir.net

Regular and Student registrations to the conference include:
  • Your individual 2014-2015 ISMIR membership fee (i.e. USD 10)
  • Attendance to all paper sessions, posters, invited speeches between 28th-31st October
  • Attendance to all concerts
  • Mid-morning and mid-afternoon coffee breaks between 28th-30th October
  • Mid-morning coffee break on 31st October
  • 3 tickets for lunch between 28th-30th October
  • 1 ticket for the conference banquet on 30th October
  • 1 ticket for the reception on 27th October
  • A quality backpack of ISMIR-2014
  • An electronic version of the conference proceedings
  • A Polo shirt (Size can be selected at registration)
  • Among other things...

About Tutorial registration:

  • The price indicated below is for attendance to 1 single tutorial (either morning or afternoon) on 27th October. 
  • Attendance to 1 tutorial in the morning and 1 in the afternoon is possible, for twice the price indicated below.
  • People attending both morning and afternoon tutorials will have the meal of 27th October included.

Cancellation Policy   
You may cancel your registration by writing to ismir2014-registration@ismir.net . In order to receive a refund, the request for cancellation must be received by ISMIR2014 Registration Chair on or before 30 September 2014, with a processing fee of USD 50. If your cancellation request is received after 30 September 2014, no refund is given.
