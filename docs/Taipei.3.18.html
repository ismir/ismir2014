Welcome to Taipei, Taiwan!

Being selected as one of the 52 Places to Go in 2014 and the No. 1 city in the 26 Remarkable Places for Solo Travel, Taipei/Taiwan is a must-go destination in Asia.

There are plenty of exciting things to do and places to see in Taipei. The National Palace Museum is one of the finest repositories of Chinese art in the world. Taipei 101, the second tallest building in the world, allows you to immerse yourself in Taipei from 508 meters (1,667 feet) high. Also, don’t forget about the sizzling night markets, 24/7 karaoke, and cool tea houses and coffee shops.
