Information for Presenters

Oral Presentation

  • Each paper has an allocation of 20 minutes for presentation, including Q&As and changeover to the next speaker. We encourage you to prepare your slides so that your presentation fits in 15 minutes, and to leave 5 minutes for questions from the audience. A chair-person will conduct the session and NO time extension will be granted beyond your 20 minutes.
  • Please make sure to include sound examples when possible and to prepare a lively presentation. If possible, leave enough time for Q&A to promote interaction with the audience.
  • We encourage presenters to use their personal computers for making their presentations. However, in case you do require a computer, or for any particular technical requirement/question, please let us know by email before Friday October 17th.
  • Presenters are asked to be present in the room, introduce themselves to their session chair and make sure that the presentation devices work properly at least 15 minutes before the start of your session.

Poster Presentation

Time

  • Each day, a different selection of posters will be hung in specific spaces at the venue for *the whole day*. Your particular space will be communicated to you in due time. On the specific day of display of your poster, you are expected to be near your poster at least during two time slots, when coffee is served -- between 10:50AM-12:00AM and between 3:30PM-4:30PM. You are also very welcome to interact with other participants around your poster during any time of that day.

Poster Size

  • According to the size of the poster stand, we prefer posters of size similar to width = 120cm and height = 150cm, in either Portrait or Landscape format (A0 would be too small).
  • For people who would like to use the local printing service, please leave 5cm top margin and 5cm bottom margin. The conference organizers will add ISMIR logos to the PDF you send us before we print them.
  • For people who is not going to use the local printing service, you might want to use the following header: ismir_posterbk.rar.
  • There is no template for the poster.

Poster Craze

  • During the conference, "poster-craze" sessions will be organized during regular plenary sessions, between 10:20AM-10:50AM. On the specific day of your poster presentation, you will have exactly one minute and one-page slide (time will be controlled by the session chair) to introduce your poster and "tease" the audience, inviting them to a more personal scientific discussion later around your poster.
  • You will NOT have the possibility to use your own device for showing your slide. You must send your slide (in PDF format) to the conference organizers who will transfer it on the conference computer.
  • If you are presenting a poster at ISMIR2014, please send your one-page slide in PDF to ismir2014-papers@ismir.net before Friday October 17th.
  • Please name your file according to your paper ID, for example 211_craze.PDF.

Local Printing

  • The local printing service charges 20 USD per poster. The local organization committee will print the poster and bring it to the conference venue.
  • Please pay your local printing service fee online (Registration.html), before sending us the poster. Otherwise you will have to pay the fee on-site, but we won't issue receipts for on-site payment of poster printing fee.
  • People who would like to use the local printing service need to email your PDF file to ismir2014-papers@ismir.net no later than October 12th (Sunday) (October 17th).
    • For regular session posters, please name your file according to your paper ID, for example 211_poster.PDF.
    • For LBD session posters, please also name your file according to your paper ID, for example LBD-10_poster.PDF.
    • For MIREX posters, please name the file according to your registration ID, for example MIREX-1000_poster.PDF.

Miscellaneous

  • For playing sound examples, poster presenters should bring their own headphones (no loudspeakers will be allowed).
  • Each poster presenter will be assigned to a particular stand. Put up your poster on the stand that displays your paper title.
  • For any particular technical requirement/question, please let us know by email before Friday October 17th.
