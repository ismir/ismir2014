Student Travel Grant

Call for Applications

For the upcoming 15th Conference of the International Society for Music Information Retrieval (ISMIR), October 27-31 in Taipei, Taiwan (http://ismir2014.ismir.net/), the ISMIR Society will offer a small number of student awards, whereby the registration fee for the conference will be waived. All students who are the first author of an accepted ISMIR paper may apply for this award, providing the following documents:
  • A copy of the accepted ISMIR paper (PDF)
  • Short CV (max 2 pages) (PDF)
  • Statement which clearly specifies the need for this award (PDF)
  • One letter of support by academic supervisor (PDF)

The final decision will be made by the ISMIR Board based on the quality of the paper, the statement, and the letter of support. Priority will be given to (1) students who are not supported by their labs, and thus unable to attend the ISMIR conference, and (2) students who are new to ISMIR; however, all eligible student authors are encouraged to apply.

If you choose to apply, please send the necessary documents to 'ejhumphrey at nyu dot edu' by 17 July, 2014; notification of acceptance will be made on 21 July, 2014. Please note that early registration closes 25 July, 2014.
